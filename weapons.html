All you need to know about weapons and combat is right here!

Pistols

Pistols are the weapons the protagonist gets at the beginning of the game. They are semi-fast weapons. Pistols deal very low damage to armored units, unless a specific pistol has been picked a.k.a. Desert Eagle. Their upperhand is their usage as they are only one-hand sidearm. This allows the user to use them on a zipline. Still, they are a great choice for the beginners and the ones who can aim good for the head.

Submachine-guns

Submachine-guns are generally harder to find on the field of battle. Not a lot of enemy use this kind of a weapon. They are rather short on the clips. Another problem is their accuracy and a bit worse damage than pistols. Still they are very effective against low armored targets. Currently the mp5 holds its place as the best smg there is to offer. Scorpion offers higher rate of fire in exchange of accuracy. The choice is yours...

Assault rifles

Assault rifles are the most popular choice of a weapon among everyone. Not a surprise there as they deal medium-to-high damage and can penetrate most armors. The famous AK-47 can be found anywhere you look. The downside is that AK-47 doesn't offer any upgrades for itself. Therefore, other weapons may be slightly better as they allow multiple upgrades and have more stability and less recoil than the mentioned AK-47.

Shotguns

Shotguns, the favorite weapon of an enemy who likes close encounters. Deals very high damage up close but they are very terrible at longer ranges, except the other models where shots are sprayed accross, hitting everyone in range and leaving open wounds. Shotguns are also very effective against any kind of an animal.

Sniper rifles

Sniper rifles are the famous among the hunters. They offer huge stability and the famous one-shot kills. Some sniper rifles are automatic which allows the user to shot multiple shots in a short amount of time. SVD being the most popular choice at the start. Other kind of snipers are single-high-burst shot. They suffer with rate of fire for bigger damage potential.

Machine guns

Machine guns are the heaviest weapons in the game after the RPG. Most heavy-armored enemy's will use these weapons. They are highly-deadly but makes the user move slower because of their weight. Some other heavy weapons like RPG are used against choppers and vehicles. Extremely deadly at any range. But then isn't everything dangerous on this island?
