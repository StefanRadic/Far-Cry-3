Details about some characters from Far Cry 3.

Danger level:

Information

Name: Vaas Montenegro

Profile: Vaas Montenegro appears in the game as first antagonist. He chases the main player during the storyline. Very deadly and cruel member of the pirates. He is working together with Hoyt.

Danger level:

Information

Name: Hoyt Volker

Profile: Hoyt walker appears later in the game as the second antagonist. He is more deadlier than Vaas was. He deals with huge human trafficking and slave labor. Has a group of privateers. The strong armed soldiers.

Danger level:

Information

Name: Jason Brody

Profile: Jason Brody, escapes the Vaas captivity and tries to make his revenge for his fallen friends. Tho, nobody is sure why Vaas never wanted to kill Jason in the first place. Just wanted to make sure he suffers.

Danger level:

Information

Name: Citra Talugmai

Profile: Citra Talugmai appears to be the Vaas's sister. She helps Jason recover and bring himself to become an ultimate warrior of the Rakyat. Later, her true identity is exposed. Vaas got insane by his sister as she never appreciated his actions...

Danger level:

Information

Name: Dennis Rogers

Profile: Dennis Rogers finds Jason under the water and rescues him. He is a trustworthy ally and helps Jason on his journey with useful information. Though, not the same can be said about his life history...

Danger level:

Information

Name: Willis Huntley

Profile: Willis Huntley known as a "White man" is an Agent working for CIA. He helps Jason to locate his missing friends and helps him get to Hoyt's island.

Danger level:

Information

Name: Sam Becker

Profile: Sam Becker is an agent working for CIA as well. He is from Germany and he managed to infiltrate in the privateers ranks. He trusts Jason and becomes his good friend.

Danger level:

Information

Name: Buck Hudges

Profile: Buck Hudges is nothing more but a puppet to the Hoyt. He is holding some of Jason's friends and blackmails Jason into doing variety of things, giving a promise that he'll set the person free. Unfortunately his end of the deal wasn't true...
