Story

Far Cry 3 is set on a tropical Asian-Pacific archipelago called the Rook Islands. The game's protagonist is a young American man named Jason Broody who is trying to save his imprisoned friends and escape the hostile islands after a vacation gone wrong. He gets involved with the Rakyat , the native people of the island, and in return for their assistance, sets out to help them reclaim the island from Vaas Montenegro's pirate and Hoyt Volker's privateer invaders.

Jason Broody

Play as a protagonist and try to survive in the wild. Do you have what it takes to over the island? Vaas Montenegro captured you and your friends and you'll have to somehow get them back if you wish to leave Rook Islands. Test your skills against many enemies and fierce animals. Build your reputation and accumulate weapons and treasures from the island. Can you help the Rakyat people?

Gameplay

Far Cry 3 offers many new excitements! Grab a hold of huge arsenal. Free the posts from an enemy transgression, sabotage their encampment and other resources! Help your natives to survive and take special takedown missions. By conquering antennas your map will become larger, thus bringing more view point of other locations. Also the game offers a variety of new vehicles including the chopper!

Characters

Meet many great characters as your game progresses. Some of them will become your enemy, some may help you on your jorney. Dennis offers to help you at the beginning. Take participation in side missions and help your locals with their problems. The island may have hidden agents and other excited characters for you to see. Can you find a way to join the privateers?
